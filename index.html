VIRTUAL REALITY

We are working with all the latest hardware: Oculus Rift DK2, Samsung Gear VR, Kinect, Leap motion, Google Cardboard, etc.

1. We are using WebVR, this is an experimental Javascript API that provides access to Virtual Reality devices, such as the Oculus Rift or Google Cardboard, in your browsers

2. You can see some examples with WebVR, API with these samples:

  • WEB PANORAMIC TOUR - Tetrishall apartment project using: krpano Plugins / krpano Panorama Viewer

  • INTERACTIVE 3DMODEL on WEBSITE - Renault Escape using: WebGL / WebVR technologies / threejs / 3D Content Creation

  • Photo. WebVR 360 Tour - Tetrishall apartment project using: photo cam 360 / krpano Plugins / krpano Panorama Viewer (Flash and HTML5) viewer

  • 3D-model. WebVR 360 Tour - Tetrishall apartment project using: stereoscopic cube maps generated with 3DsMax / krpano Plugins / krpano Panorama Viewer (Flash and HTML5) viewer

The viewer inside the 360 degree film, allowing to see everything around you, truly immerses you in the content. We use various 360 cameras and professional stitching technologies to implement projects for the websites.

ARCHITECTURAL VISUALIZATION

Our Ukrainian team comprises of outstanding professionals from the fields of architecture, design and technology. Given the most advanced machinery and a diverse know-how, Render is able to deliver market-leader architectural visualization solutions worldwide.

STILL IMAGE AND ANIMATION

Let it be a still image or an animation, a family house or a large complex, our ultimate goal is to put architecture on display in an atmosphere that best communicates the identity of the building and in a picturesque style that draws upon classical cinematography. We do that by combining our expertise of advanced 3d modeling and visionary art directing. Thanks to our flexible workflow and international business developers, we have no geographical limitations when it comes to an exciting project to work on.
